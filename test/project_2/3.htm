Treatment toward alcohol dependence using neurofeedback and Chat bot

(2017-03-19)

    【项目来源】微软亚洲研究院合作研究项目

    【起止时间】2017.1—2017.12

    Alcohol dependence is one of the most common preventable reasons for death and disease in the world, especially in developed countries.In this project we consider neurofeedback as the main treatment toward alcohol addiction in hospital.To strengthen the curative effect during the time out of hospital,we will use Chatbot as an adjuvant treatment, to chat with patient,monitor their mood,even give useful advice to the treatment in hospital.

    The whole process consist of the following steps.

    ♦ Collect patient’s EEG(electroencephalography) signal,analyze their craving strength.

    ♦ Show patient their real-time craving strength message.

    ♦ Self-training by patient through neurofeedback.

    ♦ Using chatbot to chat with patient and collect useful information
